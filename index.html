About Us

IABM Electropower was established in 2021. We are trusted and reliable electricians who serve customers in Port Elizabeth as well as throughout South Africa. We have extensive experience of domestic, Commercial and Industrial electrical installations, Security systems and Renewable energy(Solar sytems) and no job is too small. Our customers value our professionalism, work ethic and our competitive prices.

Who We Are

ABM Electropower is a full-service electrical contracting company that provides a wide range of electrical services to commercial, industrial, and residential clients. Over the years we have built a reputation for delivering quality workmanship and exceptional customer service.
